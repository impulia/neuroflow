# Introduction to Neflo

Neflo is a lightweight, open-source command-line application designed specifically for macOS users who want to track their productivity and focus. By monitoring system-wide activity, Neflo provides insights into your "flow" states and identifies periods of inactivity.

## Why Neflo?

In today's digital environment, interruptions are common. Neflo helps you:
- **Quantify Focus**: See exactly how much time you spend actively working.
- **Identify Idle Patterns**: Understand when and why you're stepping away from your machine.
- **Improve Productivity**: Use data-driven insights to optimize your work schedule.

## Key Features

- **Real-time Tracking**: A Terminal User Interface (TUI) that shows your current state and statistics.
- **Smart Detection**: Automatically distinguishes between "Focus" and "Idle" based on user activity.
- **Privacy-First**: All data is stored locally on your machine in a simple JSON format.
- **Visual Analytics**: Weekly activity charts rendered directly in your terminal.
- **Configurable Thresholds**: Customize how long it takes for the system to consider you "idle".
